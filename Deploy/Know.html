About Us

Our company is a sole proprietor company established on 19 March 2013. In United Arab Emirates, Netherlands, and Addis Ababa. It was Founded by two brothers Simon and Samuel Addisalem. As we are based in UAE and Netherlands, we offer complete import/export trading and shipment services.
Supplier/buyer identification, Purchasing materials from local or international markets, Shipping & Delivering
Most car importers in Ethiopia rely on our services and connection in sourcing vehicles to import to Ethiopia. We are exclusive agents/dealers for Neta V brand cars in Ethiopia. Our showroom is located around “Bulgaria” roundabout area.

Our Vision

To become the preferred import, export and manufacturing company for Africa markets. Our philosophy is in creating a long-term relationship with clients so that the delivery of their products becomes a seamless experience that promotes loyalty.

Our Mission

Is to earn our Customer's loyalty by delivering sales and service experiences with high quality, excellent value, integrity and enthusiasm. We will function as a team, work ethically, and focus on meeting and striving to exceed the expectations of our Customers.
